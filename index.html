Random Movie Generator

🎥 Welcome to the Random Flick Finder! 🎞

Follow these simple steps to get started

Create an Excel/Google Doc sheet(in .xslx file format) with a list of the movies you want to see, including their titles, categories, and viewing platform. 1

Enter the movie details without specifying the category titles. 2

Upload the file, then click on "parse" to process the data. 3

Finally, click on "generate random movie" to enjoy your selection! 4

Sit back, relax, and let the Random Movie Generator choose the perfect movie for you. 🍿⭐
